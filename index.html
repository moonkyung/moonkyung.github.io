Curriculum Vitae

Wenjing Jin is a Staff Engineer at the AP Architecture Group (MX) within Samsung Electronics. She got her Ph.D. degree in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Seoul National University, working with Professor Jae W. Lee.

Her research interests include computer architecture, especially memory management, power management, and RAS features for memory systems. She welcomes the chance to connect with fellow professionals in her field.
